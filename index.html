Ma Yixiao

Hi, this is Ma Yixiao. I'm seeking a job or Ph.D. position related to AI/robotics. I have a strong interest in deep learning, robotics, and embodied intelligence. If you are interested in learning more about me, please feel free to contact me.

Before that, I spent one wonderful year counducting research with Prof. Guillaume Sartoretti at MARMoT LAB. During that period, I served as a research assistant. My research focused on using deep reinforcement learning (DRL) for multi-agent exploration tasks, let agents learn how to generate high-quality, distributed exploration paths to make cooperative decisions for avoiding redundant work. Additionally, I served as a teaching assistant for ME5406 Deep Learning for Robotics at National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2024. I obtained a B.Sc. (Hons) of Computer Science at University of Liverpool (UoL) in 2022 and an MComp of Computer Science at NUS in 2024.

In my spare time, I have many hobbies. I enjoyed gourment food, playing badminton, and cycling. I am also passionate about video games, with favorites including "The Elder Scroll V:Skyrim", "Baldur's Gate 3", "ELDEN RING", "RimWorld", "Stellaris", "APEX", “Monster Hunter” series and "The Legend of Zelda" series. Sometime, I also enjoy delving into some computer technologies that are unrelated to my main job but are interesting.
